# GDPR Privacy and Data Protection

This section contains practical guidance for understanding and supporting compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation.

## Topics Covered

- GDPR principles
- Lawful bases for processing
- Transparency and privacy notices
- Data-subject rights
- Records of processing activities
- Data protection impact assessments
- Controller and processor responsibilities
- International data transfers
- Data retention and secure deletion
- Privacy by design and by default
- Security of processing
- Personal-data breach response
- Vendor and processor oversight
- Evidence collection
- Audit readiness
- Continuous compliance

- Students
- GRC professionals
- Privacy professionals
- Data protection officers
- Compliance analysts
- Cybersecurity professionals
- Internal auditors
- Consultants
- Organizations processing personal data

## Important Notice

This material is provided for education and general compliance guidance.

It is not legal advice. GDPR obligations depend on the organization’s role, processing activities, contracts, locations, data flows, and applicable national requirements.

Organizations should verify requirements through current official guidance from the European Commission, the European Data Protection Board, national supervisory authorities, and qualified legal or privacy professionals.
