# ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27002

This section contains practical guidance for understanding, implementing, documenting, and maintaining an Information Security Management System based on ISO/IEC 27001 and supported by the control guidance in ISO/IEC 27002.

## Important Notice

This material is provided for education and general implementation guidance.

It does not replace official ISO publications, certification-body requirements, legal advice, regulatory guidance, or professional consulting services.

Organizations should obtain the current authorized editions of ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27002 and verify requirements with qualified legal, compliance, security, audit, and certification professionals.
